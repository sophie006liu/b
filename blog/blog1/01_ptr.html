Peach Tree Rascals - "Ultra" (2018)


Experienced: Saturday October 8th, 2022 at CMU Wiegand Gym

I saw peach tree rascals in concert today!

absolute banger

I got lucky and won VIP tickets so I could sit in the front row. I basically got to sing with them. I never thought I would be able to experience this; It's like singing your favorite songs but with the person who wrote the song! Seeing them smile as i sang along with them was amazing! These songs I have connected to, felt comfort in, and experienced my life with, felt completely different when the creator of the song allowed me to experience the auditory journey with them.

I don't think I have been happier in this semester. As I am editing this one month later, I can say this is still true. That concert was a once in a lifetime experience, when I listen to their songs now, I remember the concert, singing along with them, and I feel another level of connection to the songs.

I first found out about PTR during quarantine when I was listening to new music on Youtube. Then, I started listening to more and mroe of their songs like Violet, Summa, Ultra, Plus, Cranberry, Water, and Glide. I went through a lot mentally during quarantine but listening to their songs helped me relax and take a step back from everything.

On another note, Tarrek followed me on insta which is UNREAL. He is my only follower, and I plan to keep it that way.
